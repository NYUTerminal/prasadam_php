PRASADAM AS A COMPANY WE BELIEVE
Prasadam Industries , proposes to manufacture eco-friendly, 100% bio-degradable, recycled paper for the manufacture of bags and food boxes for fastfood outlets. We have undertaken this project for chiefly two reasons. To discourage the use of regular plastic bags and other environmentally hazardous products. To help people plant trees everytime they use Prasadam bags.Our paper is handmade and manufactured using recycled paper,wood refuse and fruit/vegetable pulp (Lawn grass,wild carrots,Thyme,Tulsi etc) added to it. This special pulp, protects the seed from extreme conditions and keeps them alive for years. A tiny amount of glue/fixative added to the final pulp gives you strong,bonded paper of varying thickness.
